About Us

Smart Events is a boutique agency with over 6 years or experience in events management in the UAE. We have extensive practical experience and in-depth knowledge in event design and event management.

Smart Events has employed an international team of experienced and creative individuals who pride themselves on developing fresh and unique ideas for a variety of special events. It is our ability to think outside the box combined with the use of innovative technologies that keeps the competition on their toes.

The Smart Events Team is passionate about making your event a success. The Smart Team has delivered numerous events for clients including one of our H.H. Sheikh Saeed Bin Zayed of the UAE, the YAS Marina Circuit, Ferrari World Abu Dhabi, F1 in Schools, ADIPEC, Emirates Global Aluminum, Roads and Transit Authority and the Tourism and Cultural Authority.
